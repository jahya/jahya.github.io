About

Andrew McWilliams is a New York-based artist and technologist. He is founder and director of the ThoughtWorks Arts Residency program, the Art-A-Hack co-creation program, and the Hardware Hack Lab community workshop series, as well as a technical consultant at ThoughtWorks.

His exhibitions include HarvestWorks (New York City), Currents (New Mexico), Jaaga (Bangalore), and AlphaVille (London). He was awarded residencies at the Contemporary Artist Center (Troy), the I-Park Foundation (Connecticut), and on the Jaaga Residency Program (Bangalore). He has run workshops at the School for Poetic Computation (New York City), Goldsmiths (London) and at ThoughtWorks (New York).

Artist Statement

"My work explores what we miss because of how we are constructed — biologically, socially and psychologically. Using technology to generate and manipulate media, I create situational environments which question the fundamentals of how we navigate, make sense of, and relate to the world."
