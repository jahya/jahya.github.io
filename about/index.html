I am a program leader experienced in emerging technologies, software development, art production, and tech sector sustainability.

I create and lead groundbreaking programs intersecting technology with practitioner communities to address important issues, including in emerging technologies, climate change, transhumanism, AI and robotics.

The global community of ClimateAction.tech, whose 10,000+ members advocate for sustainability in the tech sector, was founded in 2017 and now operates under independent global tech-sector leadership.

Thoughtworks Arts, an incubator for artist-led emerging technology collaborations, activated more than 50 projects with over 400 contributors over seven years.

These and other programs I've created and led work by building momentum within practitioner communities, working across functions, roles and domains, and enabling team members to find authentic expression through their contributions.

With two decades experience as a hands-on artist and technologist, I produce my own projects and those of many others. My core mission is to propel projects and programs around shared identity in which we as practitioners see ourselves.

I believe in the power of mapping our underlying personal motivations to the activities we engage in to supercharge teams and produce effective and surprising outcomes.
